Here's a quick intro to Dart Frogs in captivity:

    The most common species kept in captivity are:
  • D. tinctorius
  • D. leucomelas
  • D. auratus
  • E. tricolor
  • R. vanzolinii
  • R. imitator
  • R. ventrimaculata
  • R. variablis
  • R. lamasi
  • R. amazonica
  • P. terribles
  • P. biocolor
  • P. vittatus

Some of the more uncommon species of Dart Frogs kept in captivity are:

  • O. pumilo
  • O. sylvatica
  • O. histrionica
  • O. granulifera
  • O. vicentei
  • R. benedicta
  • R. fantastica

The three species most recommended for beginners are: D. leucomelas, D. tinctorius, and D. auratus. These species are often available at reptile shows, online, and at some specialty stores. These species are easy to take care of, and there are a multitude of morphs available on the web.
