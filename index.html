The Dendrobatidae family is a fascinating family and with proper care,you can keep these amazing animals in your very own home! The Dendrobatidae family is composed of 13 genera, including the Dendrobates, the Phyllobates, the Oophaga, and many others.

    The most common species kept in captivity are:
  • D. tinctorius
  • D. leucomelas
  • E. tricolor
  • R. vanzolinii
  • R. imitator
  • R. ventrimaculata
  • R. variablis
  • R. lamasi
  • R. amazonica
  • P. terribles
  • P. biocolor
  • P. vittatus

Some of the more uncommon species of Dart Frogs kept in captivity are:

  • O. pumilo
  • O. sylvatica
  • O. histrionica
  • O. granulifera
  • O. vicentei
  • R. benedicta
  • R. fantastica
